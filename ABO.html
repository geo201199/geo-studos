About ABCO

Welcome to ABCO, the Anti Bullying Covert Operation. Our mission is to create a safer environment for students by tackling bullying head-on through strategic training, awareness, and communication. Founded in Dover, UK, we aim to provide a positive and supportive space where everyone feels empowered to speak up and seek help.

What We Do

  • Training: We offer free training sessions for students, teachers, and staff to recognize and address bullying effectively.
  • Support: ABCO provides resources and support for those affected by bullying, offering a safe and confidential place to share their experiences.
  • Communication: We focus on improving communication in schools, ensuring that no student's voice goes unheard.
  • Expansion: We plan to expand ABCO beyond Dover to reach more schools across the UK.
