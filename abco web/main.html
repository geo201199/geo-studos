About ABCO

Welcome to ABCO, the Anti Bullying Covert Operation. Our mission is to create a safer environment for students by tackling bullying head-on through strategic training, awareness, and communication.

What We Do

  • Training: Free training sessions for students, teachers, and staff.
  • Support: Safe, confidential resources for those affected by bullying.
  • Communication: Ensuring no student's voice goes unheard.
