Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions we receive:

  • What should I do if I see someone being bullied?
    It's important to speak up and report the incident to a trusted adult or authority figure.
  • How can I get involved with A.B.O.?
    You can volunteer, donate, or attend our events. Check our Join Us page for more information.
  • Is my conversation confidential?
    Yes, all conversations with our support team are confidential and safe.
